3 Mockup redesigns

The Comp 1950 site design is composed of three functional areas: Home page, lecture pages, and blog. Imforming and guiding the redesign was information provided to Team Ajax in the form of questionnaire responses. Major considerations for the design include:

  • Chronological or Topic oriented lecture pages
  • Ease of access and intuitive navigation
  • Search Functionality
  • Relatively clean design style
  • Context relevant resources section (discussion, blog, code examples, exercises, links, concepts etc)

Other design considerations included:

  • Value of topic introduction and lecture number
  • Most effective placement of images and code examples
  • Whether to use animations or carousels, and specifically using modern technologies like flash
